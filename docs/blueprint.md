# **App Name**: ANAVA STUDIO Landing Page

## Core Features:

- Hero Section Display: Showcase a compelling title, subtitle, and primary call-to-action to engage visitors immediately.
- Value Proposition Section: Detail ANAVA STUDIO's core value through three distinct blocks emphasizing analysis, custom design, and reliability.
- Services Overview: List the specialized services offered, such as custom web applications, management tools, and tailored business solutions.
- Projects Showcase: Present a curated selection of past projects with app name, brief description, and relevant tags, displayed as interactive cards.
- Approach Section: Outline the studio's five-step working methodology, from analysis to follow-up, ensuring clarity and transparency.
- Contact Form: Provide a simple contact form (Name, Email, Message) for visitors to inquire about projects. Form submission will trigger an email notification.
- Responsive Layout and Navigation: Ensure the landing page adapts seamlessly to various screen sizes (mobile, tablet, desktop) and includes smooth-scrolling navigation.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: A sophisticated, deep blue-grey (#2C5D6C). Chosen for its professional yet calming influence, reflecting reliability and depth for a digital studio with a human touch, contrasting beautifully with a light background.
- Background Color: A very light, subtle off-white with a hint of blue-grey (#F6F8F9). This maintains the requested 'light background' while adding warmth and preventing a sterile feel, aligning with the primary hue.
- Accent Color: A vibrant, clear sky blue (#61A4AE). This color is analogous to the primary and offers a brighter, more energetic contrast, suitable for calls-to-action and key highlights without overpowering the professional tone.
- Headlines will use 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif), lending a modern, tech-savvy yet clean aesthetic. Body text will use 'Inter' (sans-serif), ensuring readability and a neutral, objective feel for professional content.
- Utilize minimalist, clean line-art icons across all sections to reinforce the studio's focus on simplicity and modern design, ensuring consistency with the overall aesthetic.
- Implement a spacious, section-based layout with generous white space to enhance readability and achieve a minimalist, modern feel. Sections will be clearly demarcated to guide the user's eye.
- Incorporate subtle scroll-triggered animations for content entrance and smooth transitions for section navigation, enhancing the user experience without being distracting. This ensures the 'smooth scrolling' requirement is met gracefully.
